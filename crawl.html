Welcome to crawlJS! use the numpad to move - you can move up, down, left, right, or diagonally. use Q and W to rotate your sword clockwise or counter-clockwise. The play area must be selected for the controls to work.

Fight monsters by navigating your sword onto them. If there is room, you will knock backwards and may stun them. Stunned enemies appear in lower case and are easier to kill.

Striking an enemy costs STAMINA. Try not to run out - doing so can cost you a turn. Take a turn off to regain stamina by pressing '5'

Some enemies may become more aggressive when harmed... Some enemies are more predictable than others...

If you make a mistake, press BACKSPACE to undo an aciton. You can travel up to 10 turns into the past in this way.
